Dearest Mama & Papa,

A few words of gratitude for everything you’ve done

As I sit down to write this, I find myself overwhelmed by how much has changed over the years, yet how deeply your love has remained the constant foundation of my life. There are so many moments in a day that pass by unsaid, so many small sacrifices that go unnoticed, and I wanted to take this moment to simply say: Thank you.

Looking back at the memories we’ve shared, I realize now just how much patience and strength it took to raise me. You were my first teachers, my safest harbor, and my biggest cheerleaders. Whether it was helping me through difficult times or celebrating my smallest victories, your support never wavered.

I appreciate the early mornings, the late nights, the lessons you taught me not just with words but with your actions. You showed me what kindness looks like, what resilience means, and how to love without condition. I am who I am today because of you.

I want to promise you that I will carry these values forward. I hope to make you proud, not just through my achievements, but by being the kind, compassionate person you raised me to be. Please know that every day, I am grateful for the gift of having you as my parents.

Your loving son,
John Rey A. Castillo
December 18, 2025
